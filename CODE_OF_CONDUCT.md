# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community.

Examples of positive behavior:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Giving actionable feedback
- Assuming good intent
- Focusing on reusable, safe contributions

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or discrimination
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Posting secrets, credentials, or sensitive data
- Repeated disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions/comments that violate this policy.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and discussions.
